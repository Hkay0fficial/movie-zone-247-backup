🗑️ Account

Delete Your Account

You can permanently delete your THE MOVIE ZONE 24/7 account and all associated data at any time.

⚠️ This action is permanent and cannot be undone. Deleting your account will remove all your data from our systems within 30 days. You will lose access to the app and any account-specific content.

📱 Option 1 — Delete via the App

1

Open the App

Launch THE MOVIE ZONE 24/7 on your Android device.

2

Go to Profile

Tap your profile icon or the menu icon to access your account settings.

3

Find Account Settings

Navigate to Settings → Account → Delete Account.

4

Confirm Deletion

Tap "Delete Account" and confirm your choice. Your account and data will be permanently deleted within 30 days.

Data Type Action on Deletion
📧 Email address ✓ Permanently deleted
👤 Profile information ✓ Permanently deleted
📺 Watch history ✓ Permanently deleted
🔔 Notification preferences ✓ Permanently deleted
🔐 Authentication credentials ✓ Permanently deleted

✉️ Option 2 — Request via Email

If you cannot access the app, you can request account deletion by emailing us directly. Include your registered email address in the request. We will process your deletion within 30 days.
