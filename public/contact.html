Frequently Asked Questions

How do I create an account?
Download THE MOVIE ZONE 24/7 from Google Play, open the app, and tap "Sign Up". Enter your email address to create your account and start watching.
Why can't I log in to my account?
Try resetting your password via the "Forgot Password" option on the login screen. If the problem persists, contact us at Sserunkumaharuna01b@gmail.com with your registered email address.
Is the app free to use?
Yes! THE MOVIE ZONE 24/7 is free to download on Google Play. Check the app for current access plans and available content.
How do I delete my account?
You can delete your account from within the app (Settings → Account → Delete Account) or by visiting our Account Deletion page and sending an email request.
The video isn't playing. What should I do?
Try these steps: (1) Check your internet connection, (2) Close and reopen the app, (3) Clear the app cache in Android Settings → Apps → THE MOVIE ZONE 24/7, (4) If still not working, email us with your device model and Android version.
How do I turn off notifications?
Go to your Android Settings → Apps → THE MOVIE ZONE 24/7 → Notifications and toggle them off. You can also manage notifications within the app settings.
