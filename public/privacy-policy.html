👋 Welcome to THE MOVIE ZONE 24/7. We are committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about how we handle your personal data. This policy explains what we collect, why we collect it, and how you can control it.

We collect the following types of information when you use our app:

  • Usage Data: App interactions such as movies viewed and time spent, to improve recommendations.

We use your information exclusively to:

3. Data Storage & Security

Your data is stored securely using Google Firebase, a trusted cloud platform that implements industry-standard security measures including:

  • Encryption in transit (HTTPS/TLS)
  • Encryption at rest for stored data
  • Firebase Authentication for secure login
  • Firestore security rules to restrict data access

4. Third-Party Services

Our app uses the following third-party services, each with their own privacy policies:

  • Google Play Services — App distribution and updates

5. Data Retention

We retain your personal data for as long as your account is active. If you delete your account, your personal data will be permanently removed from our systems within 30 days.

6. Children's Privacy

THE MOVIE ZONE 24/7 is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have inadvertently collected such information, please contact us immediately.

You have the following rights regarding your personal data:

To exercise these rights, please visit our Account Deletion page or contact us directly.
